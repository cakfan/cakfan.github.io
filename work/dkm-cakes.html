DKM Cakes & Cookies

E-commerce website that helped a local Jember bakery reach more customers online.

Live Site

Business Context

DKM Cakes & Cookies is a premium halal bakery in Jember, East Java, operating since 2013. They needed a professional online presence to showcase their products, share their story, and make it easy for customers to find location, hours, and contact information — replacing scattered social media posts with a single, trustworthy destination.

Approach & Decisions

Built a multi-page static site with a dark, elegant aesthetic that reflects the brand's premium positioning. Used responsive design to ensure the site works seamlessly on mobile (where most local customers browse) and desktop. Structured content around product categories, business story, and contact/location — prioritizing the information local customers actually need.

Challenges

  • —Balancing a premium visual feel with fast load times on mobile networks in Jember
  • —Creating a design system that feels distinct from generic bakery templates while staying true to the brand
  • —Ensuring Google Maps integration and contact info are immediately accessible for walk-in customers

Results

0+
Years in business

Delivered a production site that gives DKM Cakes a professional, trustworthy online presence. The site serves as the single source of truth for product offerings, location, and business hours — replacing fragmented social media as the primary customer touchpoint.
